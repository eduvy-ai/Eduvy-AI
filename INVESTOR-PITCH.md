## THE PROBLEM (2 minutes)

India has the largest student population on the planet — **280 million K–12 learners**.

Yet our education system has three fundamental cracks:

**1. The Tutor Gap**
Only the top 15% of students — those in metro cities with wealthy families — have access to quality personalised coaching. The remaining 85% are left to figure it out alone.

**2. The Language Wall**
Over 70% of India's students study in regional mediums — Hindi, Gujarati, Tamil, Telugu, Marathi, Bengali. But almost every EdTech product talks to them only in English. That is not personalisation. That is exclusion.

**3. The Engagement Collapse**
Traditional EdTech is passive — watch a video, read a PDF. Students drop off in weeks. There is no community, no competition, no skin in the game.

These three problems have a combined economic cost of over **₹2 lakh crore in lost human potential** every year.

---

## THE SOLUTION (3 minutes)

**Eduvy-AI is India's first truly vernacular AI learning companion** — not a course platform, not a video library. A living, thinking, personalised education system.

Here is what makes us different:

### 1. Language-First AI
Every single AI response — every explanation, every quiz, every feedback — is delivered in the student's chosen language. All 11 major Indian languages. CBSE, ICSE, and every major state board. This is not a feature. This is our foundation.

### 2. Personal AI Tutor
Our Socratic AI tutor doesn't give answers. It guides. It asks. It challenges. It builds thinking — the same way a great teacher would. Available 24 hours a day, 7 days a week, on a ₹5,000 Android phone.

### 3. Smart Notebook
Students upload their actual textbooks. Our AI masters the content and becomes an expert on that specific book — answering questions, generating summaries, creating study guides. Like having a private research assistant for every subject.

### 4. Social & Competitive Layer — Our Moat
This is where we go beyond every competitor:

- **Sathi Study Squads** — AI-matched study groups by subject. Students teach each other, earn XP, and unlock rewards. Teaching is the best form of learning.

- **Bhool Bazaar** — The Mistake Marketplace. Students save their wrong answers, understand why they were wrong, and publish them as learning cards for peers to collect. We turned failure into a social currency. We call it "Bhool Coins."

- **Muqabla Battles** — Student vs Student quiz battles. School vs School leaderboards. AI generates fresh 5-question battles in real time. Every student becomes a competitor. Every school becomes a team.

- **Parent Dashboard** — A PIN-based shareable link. No app download, no account needed. Parents see their child's mastery, AI usage, battle record, and 7-day activity — in one tap.

## LIKELY INVESTOR QUESTIONS & ANSWERS

**Q: How do you compete with Byju's and Unacademy?**
A: They are video platforms with a sales army. We are an AI companion. Different product, different behaviour, different retention. Also — their pricing starts at ₹2,000/month and is English-first. We start at ₹0 and speak Hindi.

**Q: What stops OpenAI or Google from doing this?**
A: They build platforms. We build curriculum-aligned, board-specific, language-specific, India-specific products. Google Translate and ChatGPT don't know the difference between GSEB and CBSE Class 10 Science. We do. And our social layer — Bhool Bazaar, Muqabla — creates network effects they can't replicate overnight.

**Q: Why will students pay ₹249/month when there's a free tier?**
A: Because the free tier builds the habit. Bhool Bazaar and Muqabla are free — they are viral acquisition. When a student hits the 10 AI calls/day limit before an exam, upgrading is a reflex, not a decision.

**Q: How do you handle AI costs?**
A: We route by plan. Free and Basic users go to Groq (Llama — extremely cheap, ~$0.0009/1K tokens). Pro/Premium go to Gemini or user-chosen provider. Our estimated AI cost per active user per month is under ₹8.

**Q: What about data privacy for children?**
A: No PII sent to AI providers — only anonymised academic queries. JWT auth, server-side AI proxy (no API keys in browser), rate limiting, IDOR protection on every endpoint. We comply with India's DPDP Act 2023 framework.

**Q: Is the product actually built or is this a prototype?**
A: It is production-ready. Full backend (FastAPI + PostgreSQL), full frontend (React + Vite), 4 AI providers integrated, 13 tabs and labs, social features, parent dashboard — all live. We are not here asking you to fund a deck. We are here to scale what already works.
