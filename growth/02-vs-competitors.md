This is the #1 question you will face. Here is the complete answer.

|---------|-------------------|--------|

| **Exam prediction** | No | AI predicts high-probability board exam topics |
| **Price** | Free (limited) / $20/month | Free (10 AI calls/day) / ₹99–₹499/month |
| **Child safety** | No restrictions — can discuss anything | Education-only AI; off-topic requests blocked |

### The Honest Summary
> ChatGPT is a general-purpose AI. VidyAI is a **specialist education AI built for India**. Just like you wouldn't go to a general doctor for heart surgery, a student shouldn't use a general AI for board exam preparation.

### The Demo That Wins Every Time
Ask both the same question:
> "Class 10 GSEB Science mein prakash ki avansho ka pratibimb samjhao" (Explain reflection of light for GSEB Class 10 in Hindi)

- ChatGPT: Will likely answer in English with generic physics concepts
- VidyAI: Answers in Hindi, references GSEB curriculum, includes exam-relevant points
