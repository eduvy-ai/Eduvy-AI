## 4. What Makes VidyAI Unique — The 5-Point Story

### Point 1: Language First
Every single word the AI writes is in the student's chosen language. Hindi, Gujarati, Tamil, Marathi, Telugu, Kannada, Bengali, Punjabi, Odia, Urdu. Not a translation. The AI *thinks* and *responds* in that language.

**Say this to students:**
> "Agar tum Gujarati medium ho toh VidyAI Gujarati mein padhaata hai — English mein translate karke nahi, seedha Gujarati mein."

### Point 2: Board-Aware Intelligence
VidyAI knows the difference between CBSE Class 10 Science and GSEB Class 10 Science. The quiz questions, explanations, and exam predictions are all based on your actual board and syllabus — not generic content from the internet.

### Point 3: Everything in One App
Students currently use:
- Google for searching
- YouTube for video lessons
- WhatsApp for doubt sharing
- Separate apps for practice tests
- Nothing for mental wellness

VidyAI replaces all of these. One app. One login.

### Point 4: Social + Competitive Learning
Students don't want to study alone. VidyAI has:
- **Sathi Squads** — study groups with AI-powered doubt answering
- **Muqabla Battles** — challenge your classmates and other school students
- **Bhool Bazaar** — earn coins by turning your mistakes into learning cards for others

### Point 5: Mental Wellness Built In
Exam anxiety is real. The **Mental Wellness Coach** inside VidyAI addresses exam stress, fear, and motivation — in the student's own language. No other education app in India does this.

## 6. Objection Handling

### "My child can just use ChatGPT — it's free"
> "ChatGPT is a general tool. Ask it a GSEB Class 10 question in Gujarati and it will either answer in English or give you answers based on American textbooks. VidyAI is built specifically for Indian boards and Indian languages. It also tracks mastery, quizzes the student, and has social features — ChatGPT does none of that."

### "We already have BYJU'S"
> "BYJU'S shows pre-recorded videos. VidyAI is interactive — your child types a question at 11pm and gets a personalized answer in 3 seconds in their own language. Plus VidyAI costs ₹99–₹499/month vs BYJU'S ₹2,000–₹5,000/month."

### "How is it safe for kids?"
> "All AI responses are filtered. VidyAI does not allow off-topic conversations — students can only discuss academic topics. Parent Dashboard lets you view all activity with a PIN. No social media connectivity. See our security document for full details."

### "We don't have a smartphone"
> "VidyAI works in any browser — Chrome on any Android phone, even a ₹5,000 phone. No app download needed. Works on slow internet too."
