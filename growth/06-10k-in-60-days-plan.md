# 10,000 Students in 60 Days — Execution Plan

> This is the operational playbook. Assign owners and deadlines. Track every number every week.

---

## The Funnel

```
Awareness (saw VidyAI) 
    ↓ 30% click
Landing page visit
    ↓ 25% convert
Signup (account created)
    ↓ 70% complete
Onboarding complete = Active User
    ↓ 3-5% convert
Paying user
```

To get **10,000 active users** (onboarding complete), you need:
- ~14,300 signups
- ~57,000 landing page visits
- ~190,000 people who "heard of" VidyAI

This is achievable in 60 days with the tactics below.

## Week-by-Week Plan

- [ ] **Day 1:** Set up Google Analytics 4 + UTM parameter tracking on all links

### WEEK 3 — First Viral Push (Days 15–21)
**Goal:** 2,500 total signups. Launch "Topper Challenge" campaign.

#### "Topper Challenge" Campaign
> **Concept:** "Who is the topper of your school? Challenge them on VidyAI Muqabla."
- Create a shareable challenge link per school
- Students invite their classmates to a school Muqabla tournament
- School with highest average score gets featured on VidyAI's Instagram
- Prize: Top scorer gets 3 months Premium free

**Mechanics:**
1. Post campaign announcement on Instagram + WhatsApp
2. Create a simple Google Form for school sign-up to the tournament
3. Generate a custom battle room per school (or use existing Muqabla)
4. Post weekly leaderboard updates on Instagram Stories

#### Must-Do
- [ ] **Day 15:** Launch "Topper Challenge" on Instagram + WhatsApp + LinkedIn
- [ ] **Day 16:** Begin second round of school outreach (target 20 more schools)
- [ ] **Day 17:** Post "Parent Dashboard" demo Reel specifically for Facebook parent audience
- [ ] **Day 18:** Publish second blog: "GSEB Class 10 AI study guide 2026" (SEO)
- [ ] **Day 19:** Increase ad budget to ₹500/day if CPS (cost per signup) is below ₹30
- [ ] **Day 20:** Re-engage Week 1 signups who haven't returned — push notification / email
- [ ] **Day 21:** Weekly review. Check referral program activation rate.

**Week 3 Target:** 2,500 signups, 600 DAU, 3 school pilots active

### WEEK 6 — Referral Activation (Days 36–42)
**Goal:** 7,000 total signups. Referral flywheel kicking in.

#### Focus: Get Every Existing User to Invite One Friend
If 2,000 active users each invite just 1 friend who signs up, that's 2,000 new signups in one week.

#### Referral Push Tactics
- **In-app notification:** "Your squad is waiting. Invite a classmate — you'll both get 200 XP bonus."
- **Email:** "You've been using VidyAI for 2 weeks. Invite your study partner — get 7 days Basic free."
- **Push notification (PWA):** "Challenge a friend on Muqabla. Send them your invite link."
- **After every battle:** "You won! Now invite someone to beat next."

#### Must-Do
- [ ] **Day 36:** Send referral push to all active users
- [ ] **Day 37:** Post "Squad challenge" content — show how to start a Sathi squad
- [ ] **Day 38:** Run a "Most Referrals This Week" contest — top referrer gets Premium for 3 months
- [ ] **Day 39:** Add 3 more micro-influencers (focus on UP/Bihar market — huge student population)
- [ ] **Day 40:** Increase ad budget to ₹1,000/day if ROI positive
- [ ] **Day 42:** Weekly review. Referral rate should now be 20%+ of new signups.

**Week 6 Target:** 7,000 signups, 1,800 DAU

## Warning Signs — Act Immediately If You See These

| Warning | Action |
|---------|--------|
| Signup rate drops week-over-week | Check landing page conversion — A/B test CTA button text |
| DAU drops below 30% of signups | Push re-engagement email/notification; improve onboarding |
| Referral rate below 5% | Increase referral reward; make sharing easier in app |
| Cost per signup above ₹60 | Pause ads; fix organic channels first |
| No school pilot response | Try teacher WhatsApp groups instead of principal cold calls |
| Instagram reach dropping | Post more Reels (algorithm punishes low frequency) |

## The Single Most Important Thing

> **If you do nothing else, do this:** Post one language-specific demo video per week — showing VidyAI answering a real board exam question in a regional Indian language. This works better than any ad. It shows the product. It proves the claim. Students share it with their classmates. This one tactic alone can drive 30–40% of your signups.
