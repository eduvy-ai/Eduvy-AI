# Marketing & Advertising Strategy — VidyAI

> Goal: Maximum student signups with minimum budget. Focus on organic virality first, paid ads second.

---

## 1. Core Marketing Principle

> **Students trust students. Parents trust results. Teachers trust tools.**

Your entire marketing strategy should flow from this. Don't advertise to students — get students to spread the word. Don't advertise features to parents — show them mastery scores. Don't pitch to teachers — give them a tool that makes their classroom better.

---

## 2. Target Audience Breakdown

### Primary: Students (Class 8–12)
- Age: 13–18
- Platform: Instagram Reels, YouTube Shorts, WhatsApp
- Motivation: Better marks, less stress, beating friends in exams
- Pain: Can't afford tuition, don't understand English content, stuck at night
- **Message:** "VidyAI padhaata hai — teri language mein, tere board ke hisaab se"

### Secondary: Parents
- Age: 35–50
- Platform: Facebook, WhatsApp, Google
- Motivation: Child's academic success, value for money, safety
- Pain: Coaching is expensive, can't monitor child's study habits
- **Message:** "₹249 mein poora AI tutor — coaching se sasta, better than tutor"

### Tertiary: Teachers / Schools
- Platform: LinkedIn, WhatsApp teacher groups, direct cold outreach
- Motivation: Student engagement, differentiated learning, parent satisfaction
- **Message:** "Free school account. Track your class's mastery. No setup needed."

## 3. Content Strategy (Organic — Zero Cost)

### Instagram / YouTube Shorts (3 Reels/week minimum)

**Content Types That Work:**

#### Type 1: "ChatGPT vs VidyAI" Demo (highest virality)
- Screen-record asking both the same GSEB/UP Board question
- Split screen showing ChatGPT giving English vs VidyAI giving Hindi/Gujarati/Tamil
- Caption: "Ye kyun hai better? Comments mein batao"
- Post in: Hindi, Gujarati, Tamil versions

#### Type 2: "Raat ke 2 baje" Story Format
- Student character is panicking about tomorrow's exam
- Opens VidyAI, asks question in Marathi
- Gets perfect Marathi answer + quiz on the topic
- Ends with: "Tera tutor so nahi raha tha"

#### Type 3: Muqabla Battle Challenge
- Show two students battling on Class 10 Science
- Leaderboard, XP, intensity
- Call to action: "Challenge your best friend. Link in bio."

#### Type 4: Language Proof Content
- Single concept (e.g., photosynthesis) shown in 5 different languages side by side
- "Teri language mein padho"
- Tag: #विद्याAI #GujaratiStudents #TamilStudents

#### Type 5: Parent-focused (Facebook / WhatsApp Status)
- "Mera baccha raat 11 baje bhi padh raha tha — VidyAI par"
- Screenshot of Parent Dashboard showing streak + mastery
- "Aap bhi dekho: vidyai.app/parent"

## 4. Paid Advertising Strategy

### Budget Allocation (Monthly — Starting Small)

| Platform | Budget | Target |
|---------|--------|--------|
| Facebook/Instagram Ads | ₹8,000/month | Parents + Students |
| Google Search Ads | ₹5,000/month | High-intent searches |
| YouTube Pre-roll | ₹3,000/month | Students watching study videos |
| **Total** | **₹16,000/month** | |

### Facebook/Instagram Ads

**Campaign 1: Student Acquisition**
- Audience: Age 14–19, India, interests: CBSE, board exams, study, JEE, NEET
- Languages: Hindi, Gujarati, Tamil, Marathi (separate ad sets per language)
- Creative: "ChatGPT vs VidyAI" split screen video
- CTA: "Sign Up Free" → landing page → signup
- Target CPC: ₹8–15

**Campaign 2: Parent Retargeting**
- Audience: Parents (30–50), children aged 13–18
- Platform: Facebook only
- Creative: "Track your child's study habits. Free."
- CTA: "See Parent Dashboard"

**Campaign 3: Lookalike Audience (after 500 users)**
- Create lookalike audience from existing signups
- Expand to similar users at 1% → 2% → 5% India audience

## 5. Influencer Strategy

### Micro-influencers (Most Cost-Effective)

Target: Education YouTubers/Instagrammers with 10K–200K followers in regional languages.

**Budget per influencer:** ₹500–₹2,000 per post (or free premium account in exchange)

**Finding them:**
- Search YouTube: "GSEB Class 10 revision", "CBSE Hindi medium notes"
- Search Instagram: #CBSEnotes #GSEBstudent #UPBoardHindi
- Look for student creators, not just big education brands

**What to ask for:**
- 1 Instagram Reel showing the app (authentic use — not scripted)
- 1 YouTube Short or Story
- Bio link for 30 days

**Pitch to influencer:**
> "We'll give you a free Premium account (worth ₹499/month) and ₹1,000 for one authentic reel showing how you use VidyAI for your own studies. Your audience trusts you — that's why we want you, not a big celebrity."

---

### Teacher Influencers
Many teachers have WhatsApp groups with 100s of students. Offering a free school account in exchange for a recommendation to their class is more valuable than any ad.

## 7. Referral Program (Built Into App)

### Mechanics
- Every user gets a unique referral code
- When referral signs up: referrer gets +500 XP + 7 days of Basic plan free
- When referred user completes onboarding: they get +200 XP bonus

### Messaging
> "Apne dost ko VidyAI invite karo. Tumhe milega 7 din free Basic plan. Unhe milega 200 bonus XP. Dono jeetenge!"

### In-App Placement
- Post-onboarding screen: "Invite 3 friends, get Basic free for a month"
- After every Muqabla battle win: "Challenge a new friend. Share your invite link."
- After quiz completion: "Your friend would score lower. Prove it — invite them."

## 8. School Partnership Program

### The Offer
- Free "School Account" (unlimited teacher access to monitor class mastery)
- Free Basic plan for all students of that school for 30 days
- School name appears on Muqabla leaderboard (inter-school pride)
- Monthly progress report PDF for the school

### How to Approach Schools
1. Identify target: Schools with active WhatsApp parent groups (most urban schools)
2. Reach person: IT teacher, science teacher, or school coordinator (not the principal first)
3. Demo: 10-minute live demo on projector — show language switching, quiz, parent dashboard
4. Offer: "Pilot for your Class 10 batch — free for 30 days. No commitment."
5. Follow-up: After 2 weeks, share aggregate class mastery stats as proof of impact

### School Outreach Script
> "Sir, I'd like to show you something. We've built an AI education tool specifically for GSEB/CBSE students. It works in the student's medium language. In 30 days, we've seen students use it to improve quiz scores. Can I show you a 10-minute demo? We'll give your entire Class 10 batch free access for 30 days — no cost, no commitment."
